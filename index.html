About

Asphalt 9: Legends is an arcade racing video game developed and published by Gameloft as part of the Asphalt series. It was released on February 26th, 2018 for iOS in the Philippines as a soft launch, and it was released worldwide on July 25th, 2018.


Cars

The gameplay in Asphalt 9 is similar to the other Asphalt games with noticeable differences in graphics and design. There were 48 cars featured in the game when soft-launched, but the worldwide release saw the introduction of 4 new cars, leading to 52 cars. There are now currently 175 cars as of the July 2022 update.


Split screen

The game supports up to 8 players in online play free-for-all and up to four player local split screen. Split screen functionality allows players to race on one of the 42 tracks available, with up to 3 local opponents with the new offline gameplay mode.


Storyline

This new version has an average download size of 1.5GB on Android, almost the same as the previous game. But it brings a new storyline, seasons and controls. There are two gaming modes; online multiplayer and career mode. The career mode has different seasons with events and tasks.
